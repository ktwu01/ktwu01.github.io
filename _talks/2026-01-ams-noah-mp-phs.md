Poster presentation on evaluating the Noah-MP land surface model with plant hydraulics scheme (Noah-MP-PHS), presented at the 106th American Meteorological Society Annual Meeting in Houston, Texas.

## Research Focus
This presentation extends the Noah-MP-PHS evaluation work into the AMS community, focusing on how plant hydraulics affects land surface model behavior and the representation of plant-water interactions.

## Related Presentations
This work is part of the same Noah-MP-PHS presentation sequence as:

- JSG Research Symposium, Austin, TX (February 2025)
- Advancing Land Modeling Symposium, Austin, TX (August 2025)
- 106th AMS Annual Meeting, Houston, TX (January 25-29, 2026)

## Research Context
The project is part of ongoing PhD research at UT Austin on improving physics-based land surface models and evaluating Noah-MP for plant-rock-water interactions.
