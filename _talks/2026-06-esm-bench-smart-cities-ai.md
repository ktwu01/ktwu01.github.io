Oral presentation on ESM-bench at the 4th Annual Good Systems Smart Cities and AI Innovations Symposium at the University of Texas at Austin.

## Talk Focus
ESM-bench evaluates whether AI agents can understand Earth System Model physics and code, rather than producing plausible but ungrounded answers about scientific software.

## Key Points
- Benchmarking AI agents on Earth System Model physics and code understanding
- Evaluating model behavior with a structured task set and scoring rubric
- Connecting geoscience, AI evaluation, and trustworthy AI systems for scientific modeling
