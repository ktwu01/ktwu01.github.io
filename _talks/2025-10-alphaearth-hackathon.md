This oral presentation showcased an open-source geospatial AI platform built on the AlphaEarth Foundation Model, winning 2nd place in the Geoscience Hackathon '25.

## Achievement
**Won 2nd place** in the Geoscience Hackathon '25

## Project Overview
Developed an innovative open-source platform leveraging the AlphaEarth Foundation Model for geospatial AI applications. The platform demonstrates cutting-edge capabilities in processing and analyzing Earth observation data.

## Technical Highlights
- Built on AlphaEarth Foundation Model
- Open-source implementation
- Geospatial AI capabilities
- Practical applications in Earth sciences
